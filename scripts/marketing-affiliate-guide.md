## 2. 自行搭建联盟计划

### 推广者注册页
在网站增加 `/affiliate` 页面:
- 注册表单（姓名、邮箱、网站）
- 审核通过后获得唯一推广链接
- 佣金比例: 10%-15%

### 推广链接格式
`https://shop2026easy.com/?ref=AFFILIATE_ID`

### 佣金结算
- 按成交金额 10%
- 每月结算一次（PayPal付款）
- Cookie有效期: 30天

### 素材提供
- 商品横幅广告（728x90, 300x250）
- 商品图片 + 文案
- 社交分享模板

---

## 3. 寻找推广者

### 渠道
- **Instagram/TikTok KOL**: 粉丝1万-10万的中小博主
  - 联系话术: "Hi [name], we love your content! We'd love to offer you a 15% commission on our products. Interested?"
  - 免费寄样品 + 佣金
- **YouTube 博主**: 科技/美妆/家居评测类
  - 提供免费产品 + 专属折扣码
- **Coupon/Deal 网站**: Slickdeals, RetailMeNot
- **博客作者**: 合作写商品推荐文章

### 合作话术模板
```
Hi [name],

I'm from ShopEasy (shop2026easy.com), an online store 
offering quality products at affordable prices with 
worldwide shipping.

We'd love to invite you to join our affiliate program:
- 15% commission on every sale
- 30-day cookie window
- Free product samples available
- Weekly promotions and exclusive discounts

Let me know if you're interested!

Best,
[your name]
ShopEasy Team
```
